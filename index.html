About the Book

The Loony Hypochondriacs! explores the chaotic world of exaggerated health fears and the systems that exploit them. It’s a sharp, satirical look at modern society's obsession with illness, driven by media, big pharma, and our own anxious minds.

About the Author

Ranneile Lesupi is a passionate social commentator and writer whose work challenges the norms and sparks vital conversations. With years of experience observing health systems and societal behavior, Lesupi brings wit and insight to this unique topic.
